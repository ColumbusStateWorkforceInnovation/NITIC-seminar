# Nautical ASCII-Art Library

Decorative ASCII art for the seminar slide decks. Drop a piece into a ```text
fenced block on any `lead` or `chapter` slide — the `nautical` theme renders it
as brass line-art directly on the ocean background (no box, no per-line bands).

Charming-rough is on-brand. Pieces run ~6-12 lines; pick one that fits the
section's idea and vary them within a deck. Plain ASCII only.

---
