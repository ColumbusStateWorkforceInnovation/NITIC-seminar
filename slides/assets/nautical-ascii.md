# Nautical ASCII-Art Library

Shared decorative ASCII art for the seminar slide decks. Drop a piece into a
```text fenced block on any `lead` or `chapter` slide — the `nautical` theme
renders it as faint brass line-art directly on the ocean background (no box).

Keep pieces plain-ASCII (charming-rough is on-brand — the repo's one-pagers do
the same). Pick a piece that fits the section's idea; reuse across decks is
fine, but vary them *within* a single deck.
